# Platform Engineering

Use this skill when creating a platform layer for teams to build and ship software faster.

## Objectives
- Define reusable platform services and self-service experiences
- Improve developer productivity and standardization
- Align platform capabilities with engineering needs

## Instructions
1. Review team workflows and common friction points.
2. Identify platform capabilities that can be standardized.
3. Propose self-service interfaces, policies, and templates.
4. Recommend adoption and governance approach.
