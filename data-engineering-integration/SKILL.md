# Data Engineering and Integration

Use this skill when connecting systems, platforms, or data sources.

## Objectives
- Design reliable data movement and transformation flows
- Support integration across systems and teams
- Improve quality, governance, and scalability

## Instructions
1. Identify source systems, consumers, and data needs.
2. Outline ingestion, transformation, and delivery patterns.
3. Recommend storage, schema, and monitoring approaches.
4. Summarize governance and operational considerations.
